Nidhi Time Table

Connect your Google Sheet below to load live data

One-time setup — takes 2 minutes

1

Open the timetable in Google Sheets.

2

Click File → Share → Publish to web.

3

First dropdown: pick your sheet tab (e.g. Sheet1). Second dropdown: pick Comma-separated values (.csv).

4

Click Publish → copy the link. You can also just copy the normal sheet URL from your browser address bar — both work.

5

Paste it above and click Load. The link is saved in this browser automatically.

6

After updating the sheet, Nidhi just refreshes this page. No code needed ever.
