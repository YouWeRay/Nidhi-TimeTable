Your Time Table

Connect any Google Sheet timetable and get a live Day, Week, Month & Year dashboard. Updates every time you refresh.

1

Open your timetable in Google Sheets. Column A = dates, Column B = day names, Columns C onwards = time slots as headers (e.g. 9:30 AM – 11:00 AM).

2

Click File → Share → Publish to web. Pick your sheet tab, select Comma-separated values (.csv), click Publish.

3

Copy the link and paste it below.

Your link is saved in this browser — next visit loads automatically. Click "Change sheet" anytime to switch to a different sheet.
